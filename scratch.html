My Time at Just Dance Studio

As a past competitive dancer for Just Dance Studio, I miss competition. Throughout a busy week of jazz, hip hop, ballet and technique I would find myself during my lyrical class. The slow music and moves allowed me to release the tension of the outside world. In jazz and hip-hop I was able to release energy and tension from the week, and in Ballet I could strengthen muscles, posture, and elegance to be applied in all of my dance.
